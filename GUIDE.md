# ZapLend User Guide: Mastering Social Collateral

Welcome to ZapLend! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to start borrowing and lending with the power of social trust on Starknet.

---

## 1. Onboarding: Getting Started in Seconds

ZapLend is built on the **Starkzap SDK**, which means you don't need to worry about complex browser extensions or seed phrases.

1. **Connect Wallet**: Click the **Connect** button in the top right.
2. **Cartridge Login**: A secure popup will appear. Login using your passkey (Face ID/Fingerprint) or email.
3. **Get Testnet STRK**: If you're on Sepolia, you'll need test tokens. Visit the [Starknet Faucet](https://faucet.starknet.io/) to fund your new Cartridge address.

---

## 2. Borrowing: Requesting your First Loan

Ready to borrow? ZapLend allows you to leverage your social network to reduce your personal collateral.

1. **Navigate to Borrow**: Click [Borrow](https://zaplend.vercel.app/borrow) in the navbar.
2. **Set your Terms**:
   - **Loan Amount**: How much STRK do you need?
   - **Social Collateral Target**: How much of the 120% collateral requirement do you want your friends to cover?
   - **Duration**: Choose between 7, 14, 30, or 60 days.
3. **Create Request**: Confirm the transaction. You'll deposit your portion of the collateral, and your loan will enter the **Marketplace** with a `Pending` status.

> [!TIP]
> Setting a realistic Social Collateral Target makes it easier for friends to fund your request quickly!

---

## 3. Lending: Supporting the Community

Explore the marketplace and support borrowers you trust.

1. **Marketplace**: Go to the [Lend](https://zaplend.vercel.app/lend) page.
2. **Search & Filter**: Use the search bar or the category dropdown (Active, Pending, Repaid) to find loans.
3. **Vouch**: Click the **Vouch** button on a `Pending` loan.
4. **Stake STRK**: Enter the amount of STRK you wish to stake to back the borrower.
5. **Zero Gas**: Thanks to Starkzap, your vouch transaction is gasless!

Once the total vouches meet the borrower's target, the loan automatically activates, and funds are sent to the borrower.

---

## 4. Dashboard: Managing your DeFi Activity

Your personal hub for tracking everything.

1. **Overview**: See your total borrowed amount and active vouches at a glance.
2. **Make Payments**: On your active loans, click **Make Payment** to repay the principal plus the 5% interest.
3. **History**: View all your past successful repayments. Repaying your loans on time builds your on-chain reputation!
4. **Live Activity**: Check the global feed to see the latest trust-based actions happening across the entire protocol.

---

## 💡 Pro-Tips for Success

- **Reputation is Key**: ZapLend is built on trust. Timely repayments ensure you can borrow more efficiently in the future.
- **Vouch for Trust**: Only vouch for borrowers you know and trust, as your staked STRK protects the protocol in case of default.
- **Real-time Metrics**: Use the [Analytics](https://zaplend.vercel.app/analytics) page to view the health of the entire protocol.
